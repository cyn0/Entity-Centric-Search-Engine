Rumours about a sequel to Rajini's evergreen superhit Baasha have surfaced, yet again. However, its director Suresh Krissna maintains that no such project is on the cards now.

Says the filmmaker, "Rajini sir and I have spoken about a sequel a long time ago, but there have been no recent developments on that front. Right now, I'm focusing on Unarvugal, my TV serial, which comes close on the heels of Mahabharatha. I'm also planning a Telugu project."

Even as Superstar Rajinikanth awaits the release of his Kochadaiyaan, there's a lot of buzz on what he'll do next, with several director names being bandied about. It looks like only time will tell what he decides to pick.
